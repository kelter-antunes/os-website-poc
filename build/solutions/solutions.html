Solutions by Industry

Companies across 22 industries use OutSystems Platform to rapidly deliver their custom, mission critical mobile and web applications - while reducing their change request backlog. Our customers improve IT productivity and responsiveness and deliver innovative applications that support new business initiatives, replace legacy applications, and easily integrate existing systems and data.

  • Healthcare and Life Sciences
  • Pharma and Biotech
  • Retail and Consumer Goods

Banking

Use OutSystems Platform to develop applications that engage your customers across all online channels. Deliver better service by enabling your employees with mobile apps that provide 360-degree customer views, security and risk control. Acquire new clients through social, web marketing, and integrated CRM apps. Take advantage of credit scoring, illicit activities detection, bad loan recovery, and many other tools for core banking activities.

More about Banking solutions

Insurance

Take advantage of OutSystems Platform to build applications for managing areas like claims, damage assessment, fraud detection, litigation, contracts, and billing. Use mobile and web channels to provide customer self-service applications, as well as quoting and simulations. Create a unified customer view and CRM, loyalty program, marketing, and cross-selling applications. Use portals and mobile applications to empower agents and partners. And all with smooth integration with existing systems (DB2, Oracle, SAP, Dynamics, etc.)

More about Insurance solutions

Retail and Consumer Goods

Add a competitive edge to your retail business with OutSystems Platform. Enterprise mobile and web applications provide real-time visibility over inventory, both in-store and on eCommerce sites. Take better care of customers with CRM integration and tools that provide quality service, products and stores.

More about Retail solutions

Pharma and Biotech

Use OutSystems Platform to manage drug development and experiments with online applications for ingredient tracking, real-time lab monitoring, project management, client portals, and shipment. Have your applications integrated with existing Laboratory Information Management Systems (LIMS) and ensure regulatory compliance (REACH, FDA, HIPPA, SOX...) Offer web and mobile intelligence to drive your sales, and help patients and prescribers get information about therapy and services.

More about Pharma and Biotech solutions

Healthcare and Life Sciences

OutSystems Platform provides mobile and web patient portals in complete self-service mode. Within the hospital, applications help manage the care of both inpatients and outpatients across all units, including integrated digital medical records and imaging, in compliance with HL7 standards. Get a clinical and financial global view to control costs and manage resources supported by supplier self-service portals.

More about Healthcare and Life Sciences

Financial Services

Boost your business with OutSystems Platform by delivering state-of-the-art mobile and web applications for operational leasing, financial leasing, factoring, mortgage, cash solutions, escrow accounts, financial modeling, and personal finance. Integrate with highly complex landscapes and big data, and deliver consolidated views of customers and portfolios. Quickly convert prospects into customers and provide them secure 24x7 services.
